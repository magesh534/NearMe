Avenue Supermarts Limited, d/b/a DMart, is an Indian retail corporation that operates a chain of supermarkets in India. It was founded by Radhakishan Damani in 2002 when its first store was opened in Powai, Mumbai. As of 31 March 2024, it has 365 stores across 12 states and union territories in India. The company has its headquarters in Mumbai.As of March 2023, DMart had a total of 12,108 permanent employees and 48,793 employees hired on contractual basis.
